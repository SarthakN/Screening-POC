# JobBoard: End-to-End Screening Workflow

This guide explains how to go from a job posting to a candidate recommendation in JobBoard.

---

## Overview

JobBoard has two main areas: **Jobs** (where you set up roles and screening criteria) and **Applications** (where you evaluate candidates against those roles). The full workflow moves from left to right:

```
Jobs  →  Define criteria  →  Applications  →  Review recommendations
```

---

## Step 1: Create a job and generate criteria

On the **Jobs** page, enter a **job title** and **job description**, then click **Generate Criteria**.

The AI produces a screening framework and opens the **Evaluation Criteria** panel. Review the results, make any edits you need, and click **Add Job** to save the role to your list.

If the role is similar to one you have already screened, you can optionally enable **Reuse criteria from similar previous jobs** before generating. The system will find a matching role and import its criteria for your review.

---

## Step 2: Review and refine evaluation criteria

In the **Evaluation Criteria** panel, requirements are split into two groups:

- **Eligibility requirements** — Must-have qualifications, each with an evaluation guideline you can edit.
- **Additional requirements** — Nice-to-have qualities, each with separate guidelines for a strong fit and a partial fit.

You can adjust guidelines, move requirements between the two groups, or regenerate criteria for an existing job at any time. When you are satisfied, save the job or close the panel to keep your edits.

---

## Step 3: Add an application

Switch to the **Applications** page and click **+ Add Application**. Select the job, optionally enter the applicant's name, and paste the application text (resume, cover letter, or written responses).

When you submit, the application is added to your list and **AI evaluation runs automatically**. A result badge appears in the table when scoring is complete.

---

## Step 4: Review the recommendation

Open the recommendation to see the full assessment. Each application receives an overall fit signal:

| Result | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| **FastTrack** | Strong fit against the role |
| **Eligible** | Partial fit — worth a closer look |
| **Needs Review** | Does not meet key requirements |

Below the overall result, each requirement is scored individually — eligibility items as **Met** or **Not Met**, and additional items as **Strong Fit**, **Partial Fit**, or **No Fit** — with a written explanation for each.

You can re-evaluate at any time if you want a fresh assessment.

---

## Prerequisites

- Create at least one job before adding applications.
- Generating evaluation criteria before screening produces more accurate recommendations.
